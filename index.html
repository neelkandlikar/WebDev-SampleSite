How to cut an apple

A detailed guide by Roshan and Neel

Cutting an apple is a very detailed process that requires meticulous precision.
Be careful not to cut yourself with the knife!

Materials required - Unordered List

  • Knife
  • Apple

How to cut an apple - Ordered List

  1. Get a knife
  2. Cut apple in half
  3. Cut apple again in half
  4. repeat steps until desired number of pieces achieved
  5. remove the core
